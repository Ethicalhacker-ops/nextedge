Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 6, 2021

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Jay Prasad Majhi ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you visit our website jayprasad.com.np. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This policy outlines our practices regarding data collection, processing, and protection in compliance with applicable data protection laws.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Contact Information: When you fill out our contact forms, we collect your name, email address, phone number, and any other information you voluntarily provide.
  • Technical Information: Our website automatically collects certain technical data including your IP address, browser type, operating system, device information, and browsing patterns.
  • Usage Data: We collect information about how you interact with our website, including pages visited, time spent on pages, click patterns, and referral sources.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience and analyze website traffic. These technologies help us understand user behavior, improve our services, and personalize content. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings.

Google AdSense & Third-Party Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display ads on our website. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website or other websites. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Ads Settings.

How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to:

  • Respond to your inquiries.
  • Improve website functionality and user experience.
  • Analyze website traffic and user behavior.
  • Comply with legal obligations and regulatory requirements.
  • Protect against fraudulent activities and security threats.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in website hosting, analytics, and advertising. We may also disclose your information when required by law.

Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your personal information, including encryption, access controls, and regular security assessments.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. You can also opt-out of marketing communications and manage your cookie preferences.
