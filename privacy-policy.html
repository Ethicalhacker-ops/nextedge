Privacy Policy

Effective date: November 6, 2023

Introduction

At jayprasad.com.np, we respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect personal information when you visit or interact with our website.

Information we collect

  • Personal information you provide: name, email, message content when you fill the contact form or subscribe.
  • Usage data: pages visited, time on site, referral page, device and browser info.
  • Cookies & tracking: standard analytics cookies to improve site function and content.

How we use information

  • To respond to enquiries and provide services.
  • To improve site content and performance.
  • To send occasional newsletters or announcements (only if you opt in).

Advertising & Google AdSense

We use third-party advertising networks such as Google AdSense. These third parties may collect limited information about your visits to deliver relevant ads (cookie identifiers, device info). Ads are subject to third-party privacy practices. For Google Partner Policy details: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

Cookies & tracking technologies

We use cookies to provide a better browsing experience. You may control or disable cookies through your browser; some site features may not work if cookies are disabled.

Data sharing & third parties

We do not sell personal information. We may share data with service providers who support the site (hosting, analytics, email). They process data on our behalf and are contractually required to protect it.

Legal bases & consent

If required by law (e.g., GDPR), we will obtain consent for data collection beyond site operation. Users may request access, correction, or deletion of personal data.

Data retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose, comply with legal obligations, or until the user requests deletion.

Security

We use industry-standard measures (HTTPS/SSL, secure hosting, updates) to protect data. No transmission method is 100% secure.

Children

The website is not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 16.

How to contact us

For questions about privacy or to exercise data rights, email: it@jayprasad.com.np

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy; the effective date will change when updates are published.
