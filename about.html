About Bijay IT Solutions

Your trusted partner in technology and security.

Our Mission: To make enterprise-grade IT accessible for SMEs in Nepal.

Founded in 2020 by a team of passionate IT experts, Bijay IT Solutions was born from the desire to empower Nepali small and medium enterprises with the robust, secure, and efficient technology solutions previously only available to large corporations. We understand the unique challenges faced by businesses in Nepal's evolving digital landscape.

Why Choose Us?

  • Certified Experts: Our team holds industry-leading certifications including ISO 27001, Microsoft Certified Professionals, and CISSP cybersecurity certifications.
  • Fast Response Time: With local offices in Kathmandu and Biratnagar, our Nepali-speaking engineers provide 24/7 support with average response times under 30 minutes.
  • Proactive Approach: Our AI-powered monitoring prevents 92% of issues before they impact your business, with detailed monthly reports on your IT health.
  • Transparent Pricing: Fixed-price plans with no hidden costs, starting at just $99/month for comprehensive IT management.
