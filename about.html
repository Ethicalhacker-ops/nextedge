About Me

My Mission

I am a passionate technology educator and cybersecurity enthusiast based in Nepal. My mission is to make technology accessible and understandable for everyone, from students to seasoned professionals. I believe in the power of knowledge to transform lives and am dedicated to providing high-quality, ethical, and original content to empower my audience.

My Journey

My fascination with technology began at a young age, leading me to pursue a formal education in IT and specialize in Networking and Security Infrastructure. I am a Certified in Cybersecurity (CC) professional, and I am constantly exploring new trends and advancements in the digital world. This website is a platform for me to share my learnings and experiences with a broader audience.

My Areas of Expertise:

  • Technology Education: Creating tutorials, guides, and courses on a variety of tech topics.
  • Digital Learning: Exploring and promoting innovative digital learning tools and methodologies.
  • Cybersecurity: Advocating for best practices in online security and privacy.
